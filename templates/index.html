Developed as part of the Institutional Risk Assessment Initiative (2026). This system utilizes an Extreme Gradient Boosting (XGBoost) architecture coupled with Kernel SHAP (Shapley Additive Explanations) for local and global model interpretability.

Key Milestones

  • Month 2: Hyperparameter optimization and model validation.
  • Month 3: XAI integration and dashboard development.
